About Me

My name is Lauren Kamalu. I earned a bachelor's degree in Exercise Science from Brigham Young Univeristy and a docterate degree in physical therapy from the University of Utah. I took a special interest in pelvic floor physical therapy and have been helping people improve their pain and function. While there are many wonderful parts to physical therapy, I am hoping to get more into the world of web and softeware development. I am currently taking a udemy bootcamp and using my skills in critical thinking and finding best practice from physical therapy to understand and learn to code. Thank you for checking out my projects.
